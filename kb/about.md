# About Visual Graphx

Visual Graphx, LLC is an Arizona-based wholesale large-format printing and branding company headquartered at 7931 E Pecos Rd, Building 4 - Suite 162, Mesa, AZ 85212. The company prints locally in Arizona and ships nationwide, serving sign shops, print shops, marketing agencies, and trade resellers across the United States.

## What we do

Visual Graphx specializes in custom-printed graphics and signage for both indoor and outdoor use. Their offering covers six broad areas:

- **Roll-format prints** - vinyl decals, banners, magnetics, reflective films, and self-adhesive vinyl (SAV).
- **Rigid signs** - direct-print and vinyl-mounted boards including coroplast, ACM/ACP, expanded PVC, polyboard, foamboard, foamcore, bubble board, polypanel, and clear or matte acrylic.
- **Decals & stickers** - clear, water-bottle, vehicle, wall, glass/window, short-term, and general-purpose stickers, including high-tack and high-performance options.
- **Wraps** - vehicle wraps (full and partial), wall wraps, floor wraps, and window wraps printed on premium 3M and Avery Dennison films.
- **Banners** - scrim, mesh and fabric banners engineered to withstand the Arizona sun, wind, and dust.
- **Hardware & frames** - signicade A-frames, retractable banner stands, H-stakes, FabricMate SEG tracks, and curtain tracks.

## Production technology

Visual Graphx invests in industry-leading equipment. Their print floor uses UV-LED and GelUV ink technology with print resolution up to 1200 x 1200 dpi, with the ability to lay down white ink, clear ink, and even-gloss / matte ink processes alongside standard CMYK. Finishing is done on a Multicam Celero 7 CNC table that supports kiss-cutting, through-cutting, contour cutting, weeding, and masking for high-precision results.

## Wholesale-only model

Visual Graphx sells wholesale **only**. To buy, customers must be approved trade members such as sign shops, print shops, marketing agencies, or other resellers. Approval is typically completed within one business day after registering on visualgraphx.com. Visual Graphx never sells directly to a reseller's end customer - their commitment is to help their resellers grow their businesses with low costs and consistent quality.

## Materials and brand partners

The company stocks materials from leading brands including 3M (IJ35c, 40c, V9500), Avery Dennison (V4000, MPI 1405), Arlon, Orafol/Oracal, Ultraflex, Fisher Textiles, and General Formulations.
